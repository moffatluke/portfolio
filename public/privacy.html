Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This site is the personal portfolio of Luke Moffat. I respect your privacy and keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the site. This page explains what information is collected, why, who it's shared with, and how to have it removed.

Information I collect

WhatWhen & why
Name, email, and message Only if you use the contact form, so I can read your message and reply.
Your chat questions When you use the AI chat, your question is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a reply. Chat conversations are not stored in my database.
A hashed value of your IP address For every chat or contact request, to prevent spam and abuse. I store a one-way hash of your IP, never the actual address.
Anonymous usage & performance data I use Vercel Web Analytics and Speed Insights to understand aggregate traffic (page views, referrer, country, device/browser) and page-load performance. This is cookieless and anonymous — it sets no cookies, uses no cross-site tracking, and cannot identify you personally.

What I don't do

  • I do not store your real IP address — only a one-way hash used for rate limiting.
  • I do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
  • I do not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone.
  • I do not save the contents of AI chat conversations.

Services I rely on

To run the site I use a few trusted third-party services, which process data only to provide their function:

  • Vercel — website hosting, plus privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics and performance monitoring.
  • Supabase — database that stores contact-form messages and hashed rate-limit values.
  • Resend — delivers contact-form submissions to my email.
  • Google (Gemini API) — generates AI chat responses from your questions.

How long I keep it

Contact-form messages are kept until I no longer need them or you ask me to delete them. Hashed rate-limit values contain no personal information and are retained only as needed to keep the site secure.

Your rights

You can ask me what information I hold about you, or ask me to delete it, at any time — just email me and I'll take care of it. Because contact messages are tied to the email you provided, I can find and remove them on request.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

If I change how the site handles data, I'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions, or want your data removed? Use the contact form on the homepage (the "Send a message" button) and I'll take care of it.
